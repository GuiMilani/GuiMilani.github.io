Bio

Who am I?

My name is Guilherme Milani de Oliveira, and I was born in Guarulhos, São Paulo, Brazil. My passion for technology ignited my journey in the field of Computer Science at the Federal University of São Carlos. However, my curious nature dates back to childhood — I’ve always tried to find my own solutions to problems. Beyond code and algorithms, my curiosity led me to delve into Philosophy studies from a young age. But that’s not all — I maintain a deep connection with art, both as a passion and a therapeutic outlet.

Recently, while developing this website, I aimed for a balance between professional experiences and personal expression. I wanted to create a space that goes beyond pragmatism, serving its function but also evoking emotions in the viewer. If this somehow made you want to also explore your creative side, I consider my mission accomplished. And feel free to use the source code.

I hope you enjoy exploring my website — it’s a reflection of my multifaceted interests and creative spirit!
